## Overview

ScriptBridge Transliterator is an AI-powered platform developed at Smart India Hackathon 2025. It converts regional language scripts into standardized representations while preserving pronunciation — enabling improved accessibility, interoperability, and language inclusivity for underserved linguistic communities.
